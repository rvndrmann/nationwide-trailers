Who We Are

Nationwide Semi Trailer Hire & Equipments is a Perth-based company specialising in the hire of heavy-duty semi trailers and transport equipment for businesses across Western Australia.

We work with operators across construction, mining, agriculture, logistics, and general freight — providing the right trailer for the job, backed by professional maintenance and around-the-clock support.

From inner-city Perth deliveries to remote Pilbara mine sites, our fleet and service capability is built to perform wherever Western Australia's industries operate.
