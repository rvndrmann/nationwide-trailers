# FAQ Page — Nation Wide Trailers for Rent

---

## Page Headline

Frequently Asked Questions

---

### What types of trailers do you have available?

We offer a comprehensive range of commercial trailers including flat tops, tautliners, drop decks, low loaders, extendables, skeletal trailers, tippers, side loaders, dollies, and refrigerated trailers. Visit our Fleet page for full details.

---

### What are your hire terms?

We offer short-term, medium-term, and long-term hire options. Terms are flexible and tailored to your operational requirements. Long-term hire attracts discounted rates. Contact us to discuss the best arrangement for your needs.

---

### Is there a minimum hire period?

Yes, minimum hire periods apply and vary depending on the trailer type and availability. Please contact our team for specific details.

---

### Do you deliver trailers?

Yes. We deliver and collect trailers across Western Australia, including Perth metropolitan, regional, and remote locations. Delivery fees are based on distance and will be quoted at the time of enquiry.

---

### What area do you service?

We are based in Perth and service all of Western Australia — from the metropolitan area through to regional centres and remote sites in the Pilbara, Kimberley, Goldfields, and beyond.

---

### Who is responsible for maintenance during the hire period?

We are. All trailers are covered by our comprehensive maintenance programme for the duration of the hire. Scheduled servicing, breakdown support, and necessary repairs are included at no additional cost.

---

### What happens if a trailer breaks down?

Call our 24/7 support line. We will arrange repair or replacement as quickly as possible to minimise disruption to your operations. There are no hidden call-out fees.

---

### Is trailer hire tax deductible?

Yes. Trailer hire payments are 100% tax deductible as an operating expense, making rental a cost-effective alternative to purchasing.

---

### Do I need insurance?

Hirers are required to have appropriate insurance coverage for the hire period. We can advise on requirements during the quotation process. Details are outlined in our hire agreement.

---

### What documentation do I need to hire a trailer?

You will need to provide:
- Valid identification
- Business details (ABN/ACN)
- Proof of appropriate insurance
- Signed hire agreement

---

### Can I extend my hire period?

Yes. Hire periods can be extended subject to availability. Contact your account manager or our office to arrange an extension.

---

### Do you offer long-term discounts?

Yes. Extended hire commitments attract discounted rates. The longer the term, the greater the saving. Contact us for a tailored quote.

---
