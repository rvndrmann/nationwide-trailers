## Trailer Categories

### Flat Top Trailers
Versatile open-deck trailers ideal for general freight, building materials, machinery, and oversized loads. Available in standard and extendable configurations.

**Applications:** Construction, general freight, machinery transport

---

### Tautliner (Curtain-Side) Trailers
Curtain-sided trailers offering weather protection with fast side-loading access. Ideal for palletised goods, retail distribution, and time-sensitive freight.

**Applications:** Logistics, retail distribution, warehousing

---

### Drop Deck Trailers
Lower deck height provides additional vertical clearance for taller loads while maintaining standard overall height compliance. Suited to oversized equipment and machinery.

**Applications:** Heavy machinery, oversized loads, industrial equipment

---

### Low Loader Trailers
Purpose-built for transporting heavy and oversized equipment. Low deck height allows safe loading of tall machinery without exceeding height restrictions.

**Applications:** Mining equipment, earthmoving machinery, construction plant

---

### Extendable Trailers
Adjustable-length trailers designed for long loads including structural steel, timber, piping, and prefabricated building components.

**Applications:** Steel transport, timber haulage, construction materials

---

### Skeletal (Skel) Trailers
Lightweight chassis trailers designed to carry shipping containers (20ft and 40ft). Essential for port and intermodal transport operations.

**Applications:** Container transport, port logistics, intermodal freight

---

### Tipper Trailers
Hydraulic tipping trailers for bulk materials including sand, gravel, soil, grain, and demolition waste. Available in various capacities.

**Applications:** Mining, construction, agriculture, waste management

---

### Side Loader Trailers
Self-loading trailers equipped with hydraulic cranes for independent container handling without external lifting equipment.

**Applications:** Container logistics, remote site delivery, self-sufficient operations

---

### Dolly Trailers
Converter dollies enabling road train and B-double configurations for long-haul and high-volume transport operations.

**Applications:** Road train operations, long-haul freight, high-volume logistics

---

### Refrigerated Trailers
Temperature-controlled trailers for perishable goods, pharmaceuticals, and temperature-sensitive freight. Maintained to strict cold chain standards.

**Applications:** Food transport, pharmaceutical logistics, cold chain distribution
